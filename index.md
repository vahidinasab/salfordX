# Centre for Research on Self‑Sustaining Systems & Societies

## Salford CROSSS/X
### Working towards self‑sustaining communities and businesses

University of Salford's Centre for Research on Self‑Sustaining Systems and Societies, known as **salfordX**, is established to drive interdisciplinary research and partnerships that embed sustainable‑by‑design and fairness‑by‑design principles, advancing the UN Sustainable Development Goals and creating tangible benefits for communities, industry, and policymakers.
