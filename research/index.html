Our mission

Research that enables self‑sustaining communities and businesses

salfordX advances practical research on citizen-led flexibility, V2X, digital twins, and dynamic LCA, aligned with UK clean power goals and local resilience.

Themes

What we work on

Citizen-led flexibility

Market design, consumer protections, DSO coordination, and fair settlement models.

V2X and EV systems

Smart charging and bidirectional operation, SoC forecasting, fairness metrics, and fleet trials.

Digital twins

Coupled power-flow and market twins for planning, dispatch, and verification.

Dynamic LCA and circularity

Time-varying carbon accounting and industrial symbiosis.

Objectives

2025–2027 objectives

  • Measurable, auditable flexibility for households, SMEs, and public sites.
  • Deploy and evaluate V2X services for bills and network support.
  • Release open tools and data for market‑grid coupling and twins.
  • Co‑create policy‑aligned blueprints with DSOs, market bodies, and communities.
